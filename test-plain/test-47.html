By the property of Vandermonde matrix:

\(\left[ \;\begin {matrix}1\\1\\1\\\end {matrix}\; \;\begin {matrix}a\\b\\c\\\end {matrix}\; \;\begin {matrix}{a} ^ {2}\\{b} ^ {2}\\{c} ^ {2}\\\end {matrix}\; \right]\)\(\;=\;\)\(\left( a \,-\, b \right)\)\(\left( b \,-\, c \right)\)\(\left( a \,-\, c \right)\)
